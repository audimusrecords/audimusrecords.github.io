ABOUT US

At the beginning we were just a YouTube channel where we published any track related to the House Music of 2015.

After millions of makeovers and receiving demos from enthusiastic producers and EDM lovers, we created our label as we know it today, 'Audimus Records'. Launching our first release in 2016 called 'Our Dreams' by Jason Gewalt, currently as Castion.

Over the years our label had greater recognition in Soundcloud, Spotify and iTunes launching track of friendly producers from all over the world, both from Amsterdam and Mexico, both from India and Morocco creating a great family.

In mid-2018, we expanded our community by creating Audimus Network, where there would be a prologue of more hasty launches.

Shortly after for reasons of the seal, it was decided to give the company an inactivity. After a whole year without activity, we return again, bringing the best of banger music as we know!
